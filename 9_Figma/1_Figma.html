Figma

Figma is the leading collaborative design tool for building meaningful products.

Figma is a cloud-based design and collaboration tool mainly used for:
  • UI/UX design (websites, mobile apps, software interfaces)
  • Graphic design (icons, social media posts, wireframes, prototypes)
  • Team collaboration (multiple people can edit the same design in real-time, like Google Docs for design)
✨ Key features:
  • Works directly in a browser (also has desktop & mobile apps).
  • Allows real-time collaboration with comments and version history.
  • Supports prototyping (you can connect screens to show app/website flow).
  • Has Figma Community, where designers share templates, plugins, and design systems.
  • Integrates with tools like Slack, Jira, Notion, and GitHub.
It’s popular because it removes the hassle of installing heavy software, works on any device, and makes team collaboration super smooth compared to traditional tools like Photoshop, Illustrator, or even Sketch
